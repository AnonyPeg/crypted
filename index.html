Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is Crypted?
Crypted uses AES-256-GCM encryption with 200,000 PBKDF2 iterations. All processing happens locally in your browser.
How does automatic mode detection work?
Crypted automatically detects .encrypted files and switches to decryption mode. Regular files keep encryption mode.
What makes a password strong?
Use at least 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.
Can I recover files if I forget the password?
No. Without the correct password, encrypted files cannot be recovered.
Is there a file size limit?
Files up to 500MB typically work well, but larger files may affect performance.
How do I know my files are safe?
Crypted operates 100% locally. No data leaves your computer.
